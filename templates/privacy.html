Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 12, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create a Spendly account, we collect basic account information such as your name and email address. When you use the Service, we also collect the expense data you enter — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions — as well as basic usage information needed to keep the Service running reliably.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the information you provide to operate the Service: authenticating your account, storing and displaying your expenses, and generating the summaries and breakdowns you see in the app. We do not use your financial data for advertising, and we do not sell your personal information to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your account and expense data are stored in Spendly's database and retained for as long as your account remains active. You may request deletion of your data at any time by removing your account, after which your data will be permanently deleted from our systems within a reasonable time frame.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly does not share your personal or financial data with third-party advertisers. We may rely on trusted infrastructure providers (such as hosting services) to operate the Service, and these providers only process data as necessary to keep Spendly running, under appropriate confidentiality obligations.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us through the contact details provided on the Spendly website.
